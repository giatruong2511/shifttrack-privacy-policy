Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

ShiftTrack (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the ShiftTrack mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

ShiftTrack does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

All data created in the app is stored locally on the user’s device only.

The app may store the following information locally:

  • Employee names entered by the user
  • Work shift records
  • Checklist items and completion status
  • Administrator password (stored securely on device)

This data never leaves the device.

2. Personal Information

ShiftTrack does not collect personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Contacts, photos, or media files

The app does not require user accounts or online registration.

3. Data Sharing

ShiftTrack:

  • Does not share data with third parties
  • Does not use analytics services
  • Does not use advertising SDKs
  • Does not send data to any server

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on the device
  • Admin access is protected by a password set by the user
  • No default or hard-coded password is exposed

If the app is uninstalled, all stored data will be removed automatically by the operating system.

5. Children’s Privacy

ShiftTrack is not designed for children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect any personal data from children.

6. Internet Usage

ShiftTrack works completely offline and does not require an internet connection to function.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
